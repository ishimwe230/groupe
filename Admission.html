Welcome to Ecole Secondary Marie Aderaide School
START YOUR JOURNEY HERE!

Thank you for considering Ecole Secondary Marie Aderaide School! This marks the beginning of an exciting journey for you and your child.
At Ecole Secondary Marie Aderaide School, we nurture a community of curious and engaged learners, supported by warm, welcoming families and expert, dedicated faculty and staff. For over 50 years, our child-centered approach has empowered students to take age-appropriate risks and develop essential collaboration and critical thinking skills. Our close-knit community and project-based learning model foster a love for learning and a commitment to social justice. We look forward to welcoming you. Come visit us, apply, and imagine your child flourishing in our vibrant learning environment!
