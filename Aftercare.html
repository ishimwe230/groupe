After School Program (ASP)

Ecole Secondary Marie Aderaide School's After School Program (ASP) creates a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment
where children can enjoy the company of friends while engaging in age-appropriate and developmentally enriching activities.
Our dedicated ASP Counselors and classroom teachers collaborate to ensure a smooth transition from the academic day into the after
school program.

The ASP operates Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, and Friday from 3:10-6:00 p.m., and on Wednesday from 2:10-6:00 p.m.
We also offer extended hours or full-day sessions during family conference days, professional days,
and spring break, providing consistent care and enrichment for your child..
